Samp Army Cricket Franchise


Home History Indoor Complex Journey Schedule Contact


Samp Army Cricket Franchise was started by Ritesh Patel and Kalpesh Patel. Samp mean 'Unity' in Gujarati language.
Franchise's journey started with a local league called Adda Premier League, where Samp Army represented for the first
time. Then it participated in many big tournaments such as US Open, LA Open, Houston Open and also partnered with Minor
League Team, Florida Beamers. The goal of the franchise is to provide opportunities to youngsters to showcase their talent.
The main owners of the franchise are: Ritesh Patel, Kalpesh Patel and Sevanti Jain

Ritesh Patel

Ritesh Patel is the main owner of the Samp Army Cricket Franchise. He plays cricket and wants

Kalpesh Patel

Kalpesh Patel plays cricket and supports the team in every tournament with his management skills.

Sevanti Jain

Sevanti Jain is a person who loves sports. He is a big fan of Cricket even though he do not play.
He supports the team and gives confidence to every player throughout the tournament.
