Project Design


Client Project's Overview

Describe your application -

This website is for my client Ritesh Patel, who is the owner of SAMP Army Cricket Franchsie. The purpose of this webpage is to promote cricket.

Intended Users of the Website -

The intended users of this website are going to be everyone who are interested in sports who may want to try a new sport. Mainly the users will be the people who have immigrated from a country such as India, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, South Africa or any other country where cricket is a popular sport, and thety would like to play here.

Overview of the Content of the Website -

Overall, this website will promote cricket in the nation where only few people have heard about it. It will also promote SAMP Army Cricket Franchise and will attract all the cricketers to play for the franchise. This web page will also attract many cricket tournaments going on in the nation, and when the players would not have a team to play for, they will consider this franchise to play with. One of the goal of this franchise is to give opportunities to young players.
